How to test

Hold the phone in landscape, press Enable motion, then make a quick rotational flick and return to neutral. The cube should snap exactly once.

Target behavior

Phone pitch maps to X, push-pull twist maps to Y, and roll maps to Z. If your phone feels inverted, open Settings and flip that axis.

Design rule

Flicks are ignored while touching the cube area.
